Rules

These rules are used to keep gameplay fair, consistent, and enjoyable for everyone at the table. They are based on standard tabletop RPG systems with a few house adjustments.

Gameplay Guidelines

Rules help determine how actions are resolved, how combat works, and how characters interact with the game world.

  • Turn-based combat structure
  • Dice rolls to determine success or failure
  • Role-playing encouraged over strict mechanics
  • Game master has final ruling authority
