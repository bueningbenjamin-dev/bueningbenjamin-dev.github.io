Items

Items play a major role in character progression and survival. Weapons, armor, and magical artifacts can greatly affect how encounters play out.

Item Categories

Items are divided into several categories to keep things organized and easy to reference during gameplay.

  • Weapons such as swords, bows, and staffs
  • Armor and shields for defense
  • Consumables like potions and scrolls
  • Magical items with special abilities
