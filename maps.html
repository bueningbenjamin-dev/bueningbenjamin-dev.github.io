Maps

Maps are used to visualize the game world and help players understand where their characters are located. They are essential for exploration and combat encounters.

Types of Maps

The maps on this site include large world maps, regional maps, and detailed dungeon layouts. Each map serves a different purpose depending on the situation in the game.

  • World maps showing continents and major cities
  • Regional maps for travel and quests
  • Dungeon maps for tactical encounters
